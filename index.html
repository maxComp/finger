Royal, Torch, Stagecoach

Vince Aguirre and Ed Graves were business partners in the Royal Restaurant starting in 1947.

Willie Schrier was one of their competitors in the Torch Cafe and the Stagecoach Inn. This competition led them to share some good reading in the personal ads of the Idaho Statesman.
